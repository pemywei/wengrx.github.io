Biography

I am currently a final year Master student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Jiajun Chen and A/Prof. Shujian Huang. Previously, I received the B. Eng degree in Hohai University in 2016. I have also been a research intern in ByteDance AI Lab, working with Dr. Hao Zhou, and Alibaba DAMO Acadamy, working with Dr. Heng Yu, respectively.

My research interests include Machine Translation, Natural Language Generation and Deep Learning.
